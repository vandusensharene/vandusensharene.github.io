About

I spent a decade managing multi-million-dollar OEM accounts — Honda, Ford, GM, Stellantis, Toyota, and Subaru — at a global Tier 1 supplier before moving into operations analytics. That background means I don't just build dashboards; I build them for the people who have to defend the numbers in the room.

I completed a Data Analytics bootcamp (Lawrence Technological University / Springboard) covering business statistics, Excel, SQL, Python, and Tableau, and I run Vantage Operations LLC, a consulting practice helping small and mid-size businesses close the gap between day-to-day operations and the data needed to run them well.

Everything on this site uses synthetic or public data — no proprietary or client information appears in any project.
